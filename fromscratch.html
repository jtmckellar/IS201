What are the steps for becoming a good photographer?

  1. Acquire a camera.
    • It must be able to shoot full manual (have complete control over ISO, shutterspeed, and aperture)
    • It must have a lens
    • It must not be a cheap point-and-shoot, but it doesn't have to be expensive
  2. Learn the basics of exposure
  3. Learn the basics of composition
  4. Learn the basics of editing
  5. Take so many pictures it's not even funny. Then take some more.
  6. Continue practicing.
